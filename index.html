A Note on Dialect
Faallo ku saabsan Lahjadda
Cilmi Press · Inclusive by design
Somali is spoken across many communities — in Somalia, Somaliland, the Somali Region of Ethiopia, Djibouti, Kenya, and diaspora communities worldwide. Like all living languages, it has natural variations in vocabulary, pronunciation, and spelling.
Af Soomaali waxaa ku hadla bulshooyinka badan — Soomaaliya, Somaliland, Gobolka Soomaalida ee Itoobiya, Jabuuti, Kenya, iyo bulshooyinka dibadda. Sida luuqadaha nool oo dhan, waxaa jira kala duwanaan dabiici ah oo ku jirta ereybuurinta, codaynta, iyo habeynta.
The words in this book reflect standard written Somali and aim to be widely understood across all communities. Where a word may be known differently in your community, we encourage you to discuss the variation — that conversation is part of learning.
Erayada buuggan waxay ka muujinayaan Af Soomaaliga qoraalka caadiga ah. Haddii erayo lagu yaqaan si kala duwan bulshadaada, waxaan kugu dhiiringelinaynaa inaad ka hadashid kala duwanaanshaha — wada-hadalkaasi waa qayb ka mid ah baridda.
This book belongs to everyone who wants to connect with Somali language and culture — whether you grew up speaking it, are learning it for the first time, or want to share it with someone you love.
Buuggan wuxuu u leeyahay qof kasta oo doonaya in uu xiriir la sameeyo Af Soomaali iyo dhaqanka Soomaalida — haddaad ku kortay adigoo ku hadlaya, haddaad markii hore baranayso, ama haddaad doonayso inaad la wadaagto qof aad jeceshahay.
If you spot a word that feels unfamiliar for your community, we would love to hear from you. Language grows when we learn from each other.
Haddaad arki weyddo erayo aan la qaban karin bulshadaada, waxaan jeclaan lahayn inaad noo soo dirtid. Luuqaddu waxay kobcaysaa marka aan wada baran nahay.
Found a mistake? We are sorry — despite our best efforts, errors can happen. Please contact us and we will correct it. Your feedback makes every edition better.
Ma heshay khalad? Waan ka xumahay — inkastoo aannu si fiican u isku deyno, khaladaad waxay dhici karaan. Fadlan nala soo xiriir oo aannu saxnee. Jawaab-celinkaagu wuxuu ka dhigaa daabacaadda xigta mid ka wanaagsan.

How to Play
Sida Loo Ciyaaro
1. Read the word bank first.
Before searching, read every word and its translation. Knowing what you are looking for makes it much easier to find.
1. Marka hore akhri xaashida erayada.
Kahor raadinta, akhri erayo kasta iyo turjumaadooda. Ogaanshaha waxa aad raadinayso ayaa fududeyneysa.
2. Find the first letter.
Scan the grid for the first letter of the word. Then check all directions from that point — up, down, left, right, and diagonally in harder puzzles.
2. Raadi xarfaha koowaad.
Fiiri shabakadda xarfaha ugu horeeya ee erayga. Ka dib hubi dhammaan jihooyin — kor, hoos, bidix, midig, iyo jihooyinka labajibaarankooda marka xididyadu adagtahay.
3. Circle it when you find it.
Draw a circle or line through the whole word. Cross it off the word bank so you know what is left to find.
3. Goobo marka aad hesho.
Sawir goobo ama xariiq dhexda erayga ah. Ka tirtir xaashida erayada si aad u ogaato waxa hadhay in la raadiyo.
4. Say it out loud.
When you find a word, say it in both Somali and English. This is how you actually learn it — not just find it.
4. Ku dheh kor u dheer.
Marka aad hesho erayo, ku dheh labada af — Af Soomaali iyo Ingiriis. Sidaas ayaad dhab ahaantii u baranaysaa — ma aha oo kaliya in aad hesho.
💡 Tip:
Start with the longest word in the word bank — longer words are actually easier to spot in the grid because they take up more space. Tick each word off as you find it.
💡 Xog:
Ka bilow erayga ugu dheer ee xaashida erayada — erayada dhaadheer ayaa si fudud loogu arki karaa shabakadda sababtoo ah waxay qaataan meelo badan. Calaamadee erayo kasta marka aad hesho.
★ Medium & Hard puzzles
Words can run in more directions than you think. When you are stuck, find the first letter of the word — then check every direction from that point. The answer will be closer than you expect.
Erayadu waxay aadi karaan jihooyin badan oo aad ka filayso. Marka aad ku xidnaato, raadi xarfaha koowaad ee erayga — ka dib hubi jihooyin kasta. Jawaabtu waxay noqon doontaa kuwo aad u dhow.
